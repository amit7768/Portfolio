One project story I love

During my Digital Signal Processing course, I was working part-time in a restaurant. It was always noisy - so noisy that it was hard to clearly hear a specific customer’s order.

I wondered: can I use what I’m learning in DSP to reduce background noise and focus more on the original speech? That curiosity became my DSP Speech Enhancement project.

You can check my GitHub to hear the difference between my original voice and the filtered version - it’s fun, and it’s real.
